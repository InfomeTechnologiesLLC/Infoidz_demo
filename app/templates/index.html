The Most Convenient Way to Share Business Cards

You no longer need to carry your paper business cards everywhere if you own an NFC business card by InfoIDz. It is specially designed with the vision of bringing convenience in sharing information during your busy schedule, which makes every businessperson find comfort in transferring their essential details to other persons.

How it works

Collecting Information

The best way to share business information is to share a business card. While preparing NFC business cards, collecting information from customers is a crucial step. We create business cards for customers, therefore customers can suggest and decide the details they wish to include in the card.
They can customize their business card by including essential details of their business along with contact details, according to the nature and mission of the businesses. Customers have the full right to customize their NFC business card based on their preferences of card design, material and the details to be included.

Material & Design Selection

Customers will have their own vision and likes about their business cards. So the first step in creating an NFC business card is the selection of the design and material of the card by the customer. They have the option to choose the design that suits the identity and purpose of their business and the material according to its utility and convenience.
Customers have plenty of design options to choose from, and they can customize their business card according to how they want it to look. Besides, we provide the opportunity for them to select the card material from PVC cards, silver cards, or gold cards.

Printing of the NFC Card

After getting into the confirmation of card design, card material and the details, the next and major step is printing NFC business cards. We print business cards on top-quality smart cards using best-in-industry printers to make the card lifetime usage.
We help customers to create a lasting impression with attractive & durable NFC business card on the material and design chosen by our customer with the details. These smart business cards are a great alternative to traditional paper business cards. Just tap the smart business card and you can swiftly share card details to other devices and the device owner can save or share them.

Handing Over the Card

The final and most awaited step is handing over the card to the customer. Now, the customers have their smart business card in their palms and they can say hi to the new world of convenience.They can easily share their business card by tapping the NFC smart business card on another person's device.
They can either save the card on their phone or they can share it via different social media platforms. The satisfaction of convenience on our customers' faces after receiving the card makes us proud and motivates us to serve them more.
