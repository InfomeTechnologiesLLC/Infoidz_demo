Faq

How can I use InfoIDz digital business cards?
InfoIDz digital business cards help you share your business card, replacing the need for many paper cards. Therefore, you just have to tap the card on the receiver's phone. The receiver can either save or share your details.
Is InfoIDz expensive?
InfoIDz is an affordable and cost-effective way to create digital business cards for all types of businesses.
Can I customize the digital business cards?
Absolutely yes, because it's your business card, and you have the right to decide the card type and the details to include. You can fully customize your digital business card.
Does my digital business card work on every phone?
InfoIDz digital business cards work with NFC technology. Taping of the business card is only supported on devices with NFC. Devices without NFC can scan QR codes on the business card to share it.
