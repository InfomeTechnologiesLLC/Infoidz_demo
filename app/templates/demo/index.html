Frequently Asked Questions

Still having confusion? Find all the solutions here, where we gather the most frequently asked questions.

  • How can I share my business card using the InfoIDz digital business card?

    InfoIDz offers digital business cards that incorporate Near Field Communication (NFC) technology; thus, they are known as NFC business cards. You can share your NFC business card by tapping it on any device that supports NFC, such as smartphones.

  • Where can I buy affordable NFC business cards?

    You can buy the best-quality and most affordable NFC business cards in Dubai from InfoIDz, the leading supplier of smart NFC digital business cards in the UAE.

  • How do I use my NFC business card?

    NFC business cards are used to share your contact details with other devices. It is a very simple and convenient way to use it with just a single tap on devices that support NFC. The recipient can save the details or share them via social media platforms such as WhatsApp, Instagram, etc.

  • Can I customize my smart business card?

    Yes, you can customize your NFC business cards according to your specific requirements. You can choose the details to be included in the card.

  • Where can I use my digital business card?

    You can use your digital business card on all devices that support NFC. You can tap it on all smartphones with Android OS or iOS.
